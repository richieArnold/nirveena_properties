Amenities At Pride Euphora

Pride Euphora boasts a world-class clubhouse with 30+ lifestyle and sports amenities and offers 80% lush green open spaces designed for modern urban living. Each building features an elegant entrance lobby, and the project integrates eco-friendly solutions like rainwater harvesting, drip sprinkler irrigation for landscaping, sewage treatment plant, and organic waste converters.

The extensive list of 30+ amenities ensures a luxurious lifestyle. Key facilities include a party lawn, barbeque deck, gazebo, pool deck, swimming pool, gymnasium, indoor games room, snooker, table tennis, multi-purpose hall, home theatre, video games zone, and a reading area. Relax and rejuvenate with features like a steam room, jacuzzi, jogging track, outdoor gym, board game plaza, and meditation decks.

Outdoor enthusiasts will enjoy the skating rink, cricket nets, basketball court, open-air theatre, and seating gallery, while specialized spaces like the elder’s pavilion, leisure lawn, palm court, yoga lawn, pets' park, and children’s play areas cater to all age groups. Pride Euphora is a haven of comfort, leisure, and recreation.

Safety and convenience are prioritized with 24x7 security, CCTV surveillance at the main entrance and lobby of each building, DG backup for lifts and common areas, a firefighting system, garbage chutes, and a compound wall for added security. Thoughtful features include name plates on flat doors, letter boxes, and a dedicated work-from-home zone.
