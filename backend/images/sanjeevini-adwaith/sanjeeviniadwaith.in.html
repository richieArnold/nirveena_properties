Unmatched Lifestyle Amenities at ‘The Adwaith’ Clubhouse

Exclusively designed for residents of ‘The Adwaith’, the state-of-the-art clubhouse spans multiple floors, offering a seamless blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience. On the ground floor, residents can enjoy a heated swimming pool, a dedicated kids’ pool, and a first aid room with an in-house pharmacy for health and safety. The 1st floor includes a co-working space, a creche for young children, and a supermarket for everyday essentials. The 2nd floor is dedicated to wellness, featuring a yoga room and a fully equipped gymnasium. Social and entertainment needs are well met with a banquet hall on the 3rd floor, alongside Theatre I and Theatre II for movie nights or private screenings. The 4th floor boasts a party hall, table tennis room, billiards room, and a mini games room. The 6th floor elevates the experience further with another co-working space, a business centre, a cafeteria, guest rooms, and a salon & spa. Finally, the terrace crowns the clubhouse with a sun deck area, an infinity pool, and a toddlers’ pool—offering residents the ultimate space to relax, connect, and rejuvenate.
